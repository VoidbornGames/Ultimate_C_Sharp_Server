# Code of Conduct for UltimateServer

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we — contributors and maintainers of UltimateServer — pledge to make participation in this project a harassment‑free experience for everyone.  
Regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation, religion, nationality, level of experience, or other personal traits, all are welcome here.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:

- Use inclusive, respectful, and welcoming language.  
- Be open to feedback and constructive criticism.  
- Treat other community members with respect and empathy.  
- Engage in discussions in a civil and productive way.  
- Refrain from harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior.  
- Avoid posting hateful, derogatory, or inflammatory content.  

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassing, insulting, or derogatory comments or personal attacks.  
- Discrimination, prejudice, or slurs targeting protected or non‑protected traits.  
- Threats, intimidation, or abusive language.  
- Trolling, spamming, or derailing discussions.  
- Sharing private or personal information of others without consent.  
- Posting or linking to explicit, violent, or otherwise inappropriate content.  

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by UltimateServer:  
- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, comments)  
- Any official discussion, chat, forum or communication channel  
- Any public representation of the project (e.g. social media, community events)  

## Reporting & Enforcement

- If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact one of the project maintainers (e.g. via GitHub issues or email).  
- The maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit or reject any contributions (code, comments, issues, etc.) that violate this Code of Conduct.  
- In serious or repeated cases, contributors may be temporarily or permanently banned from the project.  
- Reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality when possible.  

## Acknowledgements

This Code of Conduct is inspired by standard open‑source community practices.  
By participating in UltimateServer, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.
